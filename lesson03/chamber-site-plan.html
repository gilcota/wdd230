Corregidora Chamber of Commerce
Site Plan

This name represents the Chamber of Commerce of the city of Corregidora, Querétaro, México.

Site Purpose

The Corregidora Chamber of Commerce is an organization that creates a proper environment where businesses can grow together. We strive to improve the economy, culture and local conditions of our community by facilitating companies the relations between them, suppliers, government and clients.

Scenarios

  • What are the benefits of becoming a member?
  • How can I become a member?
  • Where are the offices of the chamber?
  • What are the upcoming events?
  • Who are the leaders of the chamber?
  • Is there a directory of the members of the chamber?
  • Can I get government support for my business?
